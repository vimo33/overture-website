Our
Story

Born in the small, smoke-filled clubs of Zurich, Overture wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built on the floor of the Rote Fabrik and the hidden basements of Langstrasse.

We saw the industry getting louder but the experience getting thinner. Algorithms replaced discovery, and stadium seats replaced intimacy. We decided to hit reset.

What we believe.

Fairness

We believe an artist should be able to live off their craft. We take a flat 5% fee—enough to keep the lights on and nothing more. The rest belongs to the creator.

Transparency

No black boxes. We open-source our distribution algorithms. If you see a recommendation, you know exactly why it's there.

Small Rooms

Music is visceral. We prioritize the 'Small Room' experience—basements, rooftops, and storefronts—over massive arena voids.
