Inquiries

How does the ticket allocation work?
expand_more
Members get a monthly credit that can be used for any Overture event. Credits roll over for up to 3 months, ensuring you never miss out on value.
Can I cancel at any time?
expand_more
Yes. Overture is built on voluntary support. You can pause or cancel your membership directly through your dashboard with no lock-in periods.
Where does my money go?
expand_more
We operate on a 70/30 split. 70% goes directly to the artists and production of the events. 30% covers our overheads and infrastructure costs.
