Live4Fair
The local ecosystem is underfunded across the full event chain
Problem

The crisis in local live culture

Artists are underpaid, small venues are cautious, and the cultural workers who make intimate events possible often carry invisible labor with too little structure around them.

Artists

CHF 250

Typical small-show fee signals how far many local artists still sit below sustainable pay expectations.

Venues

Off-peak risk

Underused rooms often want cultural life, but not open-ended booking risk or unstructured event chaos.

Cultural workers

Invisible labor

Programming, coordination, host care, production, and reporting are real work that often remain underfunded or assumed.

When there is no visible structure around local culture, the people who care most end up subsidizing it with exhaustion.
