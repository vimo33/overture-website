Live4Fair
Policy shifts, local demand, and post-platform fatigue create an opening
Why now

Why Zurich, why now

Swiss cultural policy is increasingly explicit about fair artist pay. Audiences still want intimate local experiences. Smaller rooms are looking for better ways to program without becoming full-time promoters.

These conditions create room for a member-backed, local-demand model instead of relying only on venue-led ticketing or platform extraction.

Policy pressure

Fair-pay expectations are becoming structural, not optional rhetoric.

Audience appetite

Local people still show up for intimate, curated nights that feel rooted and human.

Member-backed demand

Recurring membership can reveal demand earlier and more visibly than one-off event marketing.
