Live4Fair
Community-supported cultural infrastructure
Our solution

A community-supported cultural system

Live4Fair is not a generic subscription platform. It is a local cultural infrastructure pilot where members help fund artists, activate venues, and make cultural production work visible and sustainable.

The model starts with one clear public offer: CHF 29 per month, transparent pilot rules, and a repeatable 100-member block that can be reported honestly.

Pillar 01

Artists

Paid performing slots plus visible support for the wider artist pool.

Pillar 02

Venues

Activated rooms matched thoughtfully and contracted clearly.

Pillar 03

Community

Members who fund and attend a recurring local cultural rhythm.

Pillar 04

Cultural workers

Coordination, production, and reporting treated as essential infrastructure.
