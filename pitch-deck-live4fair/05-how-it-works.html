How the pilot flow works

The first version of Live4Fair is intentionally simple. Members subscribe, artists are curated into pools, venues are contracted, events are produced, and reporting shows where value moved.

01

Members subscribe

Residents join at CHF 29 and share local genre or scene interest.

02

Artist pools are curated

Live4Fair builds pilot pools around quality, fit, and member resonance.

03

Venues are contracted

Events are matched to rooms with explicit agreements and operational fit.

04

Reporting stays open

Each cycle shows how revenue moved across artists, events, and operations.

The pilot is designed to prove one clean cultural loop before attempting a wider operating system.
