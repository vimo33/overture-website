Zurich opportunity

A city ready for a pilot threshold

Zurich combines venue density, cultural participation, and a strong local identity. The opportunity is not to assume product-market fit, but to prove that a first 100-member block can be acquired and sustained credibly.

CHF 29 pilot membership 100-member validation milestone First event rhythm before scale

Local spend exists

People already spend on culture, nightlife, and belonging. The question is how much of that can be redirected into a visible local mechanism.

Rooms exist

There are enough bars, community venues, and hybrid spaces for a careful pilot to start small and learn.

Validation caveat

The first milestone is still pilot validation, not proof that the full model is already ready to scale.
