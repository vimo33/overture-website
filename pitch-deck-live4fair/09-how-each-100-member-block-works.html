Artist pool and payout logic
100-member block

How each 100-member block works

This is the core pilot formula. It is simple enough to communicate and concrete enough to report publicly.

Revenue

CHF 2,900

100 members x CHF 29 monthly revenue

Artists

CHF 2,030

70% of the block goes to the artist side of the model

Operations

CHF 870

30% supports production, coordination, and reporting

Pool size

10 artists

Three perform, seven stay visible in the pool

Performing artists

3 x CHF 500 = CHF 1,500

Three artists perform in the monthly event block and each receives a CHF 500 fee.

Non-performing artist support

CHF 530 / 7 = CHF 75.71

The remaining artist share is split evenly across the seven non-performing artists in the pool.

Every 100 members activate one event, one venue, ten artists in the pool, and one visible cultural work layer.
