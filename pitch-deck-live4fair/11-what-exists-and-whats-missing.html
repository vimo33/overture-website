Live4Fair
Pilot readiness, not maturity theater
What exists and what's missing

Enough to start, not enough to pretend certainty

Live4Fair already has a coherent pilot thesis. What it does not yet claim is proven member acquisition, proven venue participation at scale, or a fully mature operating model.

What exists

  • Clear pilot unit and payout logic
  • Early artist and venue conversations
  • Membership framing and reporting concept
  • Four-pillar model that includes cultural work

What still needs proof

  • Reliable acquisition of the first 100 members
  • Artist-pool governance and rotation credibility
  • Venue agreements that work operationally
  • Cultural-worker capacity inside the 30% operating share
