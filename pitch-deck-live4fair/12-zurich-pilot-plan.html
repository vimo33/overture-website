Zurich pilot plan

A disciplined first rhythm

The pilot is designed to learn in sequence: secure the first member block, curate the first pool, contract the first venue cycle, produce the first events, and report the results openly.

Phase 01

100-member acquisition

No pilot event without the first block of recurring member commitments.

Phase 02

Artist-pool selection

Curate the first 10-artist pool around genre interest, fit, and quality thresholds.

Phase 03

Venue contract templates

Establish clear written agreements before moving into recurring event production.

Phase 04

Reporting logic

Publish what happened across artists, venue activation, attendance, and operations.
