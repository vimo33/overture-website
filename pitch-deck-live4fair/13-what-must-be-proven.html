Critical pilot unknowns
What must be proven

The real questions are operational

The pilot must prove more than enthusiasm. It has to show that the membership base, the artist-pool model, venue contracts, and the cultural work layer can all hold together.

Subscriber acquisition

Can the first 100 recurring memberships be won credibly enough to trigger the first block?

Artist-pool governance

Can selection, rotation, and non-performing support feel fair enough to artists and members?

Venue contracts

Can written agreements reduce risk while keeping the rooms attractive and realistic to work with?

Cultural-worker capacity

Can the coordination layer remain strong without disappearing into unpaid labor again?

Operational realism

Is the 30% Live4Fair share enough to support production, communication, and reporting discipline?
