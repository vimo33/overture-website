Live4Fair
Why the model fits the program
Impact Catalyst alignment

Why this belongs in an impact portfolio

Live4Fair is a practical local-culture intervention: fairer artist pay, better use of small rooms, member-backed participation, and support for the cultural work required across the full event chain.

Fairer pay

Artist compensation becomes a visible design principle rather than a residual output.

Urban vitality

Underused rooms gain cultural activation with clearer operating logic.

Civic participation

Members fund and attend a local scene instead of watching culture thin out around them.

Cultural work

The model recognizes fair cultural work across performers, hosts, coordinators, and producers.
