Intro

A more transparent route into live performance.

Being an independent artist often means doing far more than making the work. You write emails. You follow up. You wait. You guess. You try to understand who books what, who knows whom, and whether there is any real space for you at all.

We believe the system should not be this opaque, and we believe local audiences want their support to reach artists more directly and more visibly. Overture is our attempt to build that bridge.

Paid opportunities

Recurring, fairly structured performance opportunities connected to real audience demand.

Clearer selection logic

Not a black box. Artists should understand how opportunities are created and how decisions are made.

Direct payout

Artist payment is central, visible, and intentional, not an afterthought buried in the budget.

What we value

We are not chasing polished sameness.

We care about local relevance, artistic integrity, and building a city where more voices have space to be heard. Matching matters because not every artist fits every room.

  • Distinct artistic identity
  • Emotional presence
  • Commitment to live performance
  • Openness to community and collaboration
  • Resonance with intimate and local formats
Fairness needs structure

How artists are considered

Our goal is not to promise equal booking for everyone. It is to build a system that is more transparent, more intentional, and less arbitrary than what many artists experience today.

  • Artistic quality and live readiness
  • Local fit and format fit
  • Subscriber and community resonance
  • Rotation and diversity across the platform
  • Balance between emerging and established acts
Payment philosophy

Artists should not be the last priority in the budget

Too often, artists absorb the instability of the whole system. We are designing Overture so that artist compensation is one of the core reasons the model exists.

  • Transparent payouts
  • Fairer compensation principles
  • Less unpaid labor and guesswork
  • Improved predictability over time
